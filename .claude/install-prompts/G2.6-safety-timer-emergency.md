## SCOPE -- EXACTLY WHAT TO BUILD

### 1. Safety Timer BullMQ Job

**File:** `src/lib/jobs/local-safety-timer.ts` (NEW)

Per Local Canonical Section 9:
- **Trigger:** Check-in but no confirmation within 30 minutes
- **Action:** Push notification to both parties: "Is everything OK?"
- **Escalation:** If safety timer not dismissed within 15 minutes, support case auto-created

**When enqueued:** When BOTH_CHECKED_IN status is reached (both parties at location but haven't confirmed receipt yet)

**Job 1: Safety Nudge (30 min after BOTH_CHECKED_IN)**

```typescript
interface SafetyNudgeData {
  localTransactionId: string;
  orderId: string;
  buyerId: string;
  sellerId: string;
}
```

**Delay:** 30 minutes from BOTH_CHECKED_IN timestamp

**Processing logic:**
1. Fetch localTransaction
2. If status is still BOTH_CHECKED_IN (no confirmation yet):
   - Send notification to buyer: "Is everything OK? Your meetup for [item name] has been going on for 30 minutes. Tap here to confirm receipt or get help."
   - Send notification to seller: "Is everything OK? Your meetup for [item name] has been going on for 30 minutes."
   - Update localTransaction: safetyAlertSent = true, safetyAlertAt = now()
   - Enqueue the escalation job (15 min delay)
3. If RECEIPT_CONFIRMED, COMPLETED, or CANCELED: skip (situation resolved)

**Job 2: Safety Escalation (15 min after nudge, 45 min total)**

```typescript
interface SafetyEscalationData {
  localTransactionId: string;
  orderId: string;
  buyerId: string;
  sellerId: string;
}
```

**Delay:** 15 minutes from safety nudge

**Processing logic:**
1. Fetch localTransaction
2. If safetyAlertSent === true AND status is still BOTH_CHECKED_IN (alert was sent but not resolved):
   - Auto-create a helpdesk case:
     - type: 'ORDER'
     - priority: 'HIGH'
     - subject: "Safety alert: Local meetup unresolved for [order number]"
     - description: "Both parties checked in at [time] but receipt has not been confirmed after 45 minutes. Safety alert was sent at [safetyAlertAt] and was not resolved."
     - Link: orderId, buyerId, sellerId, localTransactionId
   - Send notification to both: "A support team member has been notified about your meetup. If you need immediate help, call [support phone] or dial 911 for emergencies."
3. If resolved: skip

### 2. Auto-Cancel at 48 Hours Past Scheduled Time

Per Local Canonical Section 9: "Auto-cancel: 48 hours past scheduled time, no confirmation -> Order auto-canceled + investigation flagged"

This is already partially handled by `local-auto-cancel.ts` from G2.5. However, this needs enhancement:
- If the transaction has check-ins but no confirmation after 48 hours past scheduled time, it should be flagged for investigation (not just canceled).
- **Modify:** `src/lib/jobs/local-auto-cancel.ts`
  - If status is SELLER_CHECKED_IN, BUYER_CHECKED_IN, or BOTH_CHECKED_IN at the 48-hour mark:
    - Cancel the order
    - Auto-create a helpdesk case (type: 'ORDER', priority: 'URGENT') flagging it for investigation
    - The case subject: "Investigation needed: Local meetup checked in but never completed for [order number]"
