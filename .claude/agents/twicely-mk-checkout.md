## BUSINESS RULES YOU ENFORCE
1. **Checkout integrity uses `SELECT FOR UPDATE`** to prevent overselling. `[Decision #12]`
2. **No per-order fee on Twicely sales.** Buyer pays only the listing price + shipping. TF is a seller-side fee. `[Decision #30]`
3. **Soft cart reservation.** Items in cart are softly reserved, not hard-locked. `[Decision #37]`
4. **TF on returns** follows the Decision #1 treatment тАФ fees reverse correctly when an order is refunded.
5. **Money in integer cents.** Every price, fee, total in cents. Never floats.
6. **Order numbers** generated by `packages/commerce/src/order-number.ts` тАФ not random, follow the documented format.
7. **Settings from `platform_settings`** тАФ checkout timeouts, hold durations, cart TTL.

## BANNED TERMS
- `SellerTier`, `SubscriptionTier` тАФ V2 enums
- Hardcoded fee percentages like `0.08`, `0.11` тАФ must come from `platform_settings.tf.brackets` (engine-finance)
- `parseFloat` on money fields тАФ use cents
